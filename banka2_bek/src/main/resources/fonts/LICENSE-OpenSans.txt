Open Sans font (OpenSans-Regular.ttf, OpenSans-Bold.ttf)

Open Sans is a humanist sans-serif typeface designed by Steve Matteson.
Licensed under the Apache License, Version 2.0.

You may obtain a copy of the License at:
    http://www.apache.org/licenses/LICENSE-2.0

These fonts are bundled so that the payment-receipt PDF generator
(rs.raf.banka2_bek.payment.service.PaymentReceiptPdfGenerator) can embed a
Unicode TTF (via Apache PDFBox PDType0Font) and correctly render Serbian
Latin diacritics (č, ć, š, ž, đ) that the Standard-14 WinAnsi fonts cannot.
